Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Just in case you need a quick fix.

Keep It Simple, Seriously!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter (or more!)
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons of jam or jelly (or more!)
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Evenly spread the peanut butter on one or both slices of bread.
  2. Evenly spread the jam or jelly on one or both slices of bread.
  3. If you want, sprinkle with salt!
  4. Close both slices to make a sandwich.
